About Me

I am a second-year M.Sc. student in the School of Computing Science at Simon Fraser University (SFU). I am very fortunate to be advised by Prof. Hang Ma. Prior to that, I received my Bachelor's degree from Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech).

My research interests are broadly in theory and applications of reinforcement learning. I worked on designing practical and efficient learning algorithms for large-scale partially observable multi-agent path finding. Currently, I am more interested in theoretical foundations of reinforcement learning, online learning, and bandits.

I am actively seeking Ph.D. positions starting from Fall 2024!
